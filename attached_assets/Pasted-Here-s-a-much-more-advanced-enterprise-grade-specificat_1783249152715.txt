We have successfully reached the next production milestone with the authentication infrastructure now operating correctly. The next development phase focuses on transforming the platform into a fully intelligent trading ecosystem by introducing real-time AI assistance, market intelligence, automated strategy deployment, statistical analytics, and collaborative copy-trading infrastructure.

Act as a Principal Software Architect, Senior React Engineer, Systems Automation Engineer, and UI/UX Specialist.
